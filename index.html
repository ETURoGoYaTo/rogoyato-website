About Us

We are a multidisciplinary student community focused on robotics, primarily composed of Electrical-Electronics and Artificial Intelligence Engineering students. Our mission is to help students develop their technical and collaborative skills in robotics through hands-on projects and competitions. We believe that building a strong foundation for real-world applications while fostering a culture of teamwork is key to shaping future innovators.

As the Robotics Society, we actively participate in prestigious competitions such as TEKNOFEST, MEB and Universities' Robot Days, forming teams for challenges like line-following robots, maze solvers, autonomous industrial vehicles, UAVs, rockets, autonomous taxis, and drones. Additionally, we strive to create a collaborative platform where students can share knowledge, work across disciplines, and support one another.
